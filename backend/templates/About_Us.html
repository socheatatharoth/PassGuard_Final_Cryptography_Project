About PassGuard


Secure your passwords with PassGuard



PassGuard is a security-focused web application designed to help users create safer passwords and reduce the risk of account compromise in todayâ€™s digital environment. The platform provides essential tools to evaluate password strength, detect whether passwords or their hashes have appeared in known data breaches, and generate secure cryptographic hashes such as MD5, SHA-1, and SHA-256. By combining password analysis, exposure awareness, and hashing utilities in one simple interface, PassGuard aims to educate users and promote better cybersecurity practices.
